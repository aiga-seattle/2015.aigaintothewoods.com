Louise Fili

Louise Fili is director of Louise Fili Ltd, a New York City design firm specializing in brand development for restaurants and food packaging. Fili has co-authored over twenty books on design and typography with her husband, Steven Heller, and she has also written and designed books on Italian design and culture. She is a member of the Art Directors Hall of Fame and is a recipient of the Medal for Lifetime Achievement from both the AIGA and the Type Directors Club. A best-selling monograph of her work, Elegantissima, was published in 2012. Visit her site at louisefili.com.
