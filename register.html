Registration

What's Included

Your conference registration includes admission to all keynote presentations, 3 hands on workshops, and all evening events, including the Opening Night Reception, and Saturday night’s Party. Most importantly, your fee includes your Room & Board. All Sleeping Lady lodging and meals are included in your registration fee. Your ancillary purchases at the Bar/Gift Shop are of course, on your own tab.

Why Attend?

Insightful speakers, nightly parties, and facetime with people making change in their industry, Into the Woods Design Conference brings together top creative teams for an immersive, backwoods retreat, and leverages the unique contributions of every guest for lively exchanges and cross-pollination among different perspectives, fields, and sets of expertise.

Into the Woods gives you the chance to connect with colleagues and designers moving the needle. Plus it’s chock-full of fun extras like hands on workshops, parties, communal dining, campus mountain setting, cabin clusters, and plenty of open discussion and break out time.

You’ll return to work more energized after three days of inspiring presentations and the crisp mountain air will leave your soul refreshed!

Registration

Due to the intimate mountain campus setting of the Sleeping Lady, Into the Woods Creative Conference is limited to 120 attendees.

Please note: Your room at Sleeping Lady will be shared with other attendees, and not necessarily with people you know. Once you register you will be contacted to inquire about your rooming preferences. If you know other attendees you would like to room with you will be able to list their names. Every effort will be made to meet your requests. Please contact us prior to registering if you have any concerns.

GROUP RATES

We encourage firms to take advantage of our group discount through room block purchases. Contact us to find out more.

*Registration fees for Into the Woods are not refundable, however, they are transferable. If you decide not to attend after registering, you may find another attendee to take your place.
