Why Attend?

Into the Woods gives you the chance to connect with colleagues and designers moving the needle. Plus it’s chock-full of fun extras like hands on workshops, parties, communal dining, campus mountain setting, cabin clusters, and plenty of open discussion and break out time.

You’ll return to work more energized after three days of inspiring presentations and the crisp mountain air will leave your soul refreshed!
