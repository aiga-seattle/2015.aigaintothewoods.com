Defying convention in service of a more powerful vision, Into the Woods aims to celebrate provocative ideas, integrative thinking, and boundary-breaking design.

In April 2016, this legendary conference is back, with three days of hands-on workshops, outstanding speakers, and backwoods creative bonding at Sleeping Lady Resort in Leavenworth.

At the heart of Into the Woods is the opportunity to immerse yourself in an environment dedicated to helping you reconnect with your creative core. Designed to bring together top thinkers and practitioners from diverse fields to share ideas and experiences on everything from the design trends, the impact of design in todayâ€™s climate, to thinking about design in the future, and many other hot topics.

Founded in 1996, Into the Woods is designed to bring together the regions top creative teams for an immersive, backwoods retreat. Into the Woods leverages the unique contributions of every guest, fully anticipating that lively exchanges and cross-pollination among different perspectives, fields, and sets of expertise will result in a richer experience for everyone. Guests have ample opportunity to get to know one another and the speakers. Our goal is to cultivate unexpected insights and meaningful connections between both people and ideas in a retreat style atmosphere.

April 8-10, 2016 / Sleeping Lady Mountain Resort / Leavenworth WA

Registration Fees

Due to intimate mountain campus of the Sleeping Lady, Into the Woods is limited to 100 attendees. Conference registration fees include all events, workshops, lodging and gourmet meals.

Conference Fee

General Admission $850 + taxes

Please note: Your room at Sleeping Lady will be shared with other attendees, and not necessarily with people you know. Once you register you will be contacted to inquire about your rooming preferences. If you know other attendees you would like to room with you will be able to list their names. Every effort will be made to meet your requests. Please contact us prior to registering if you have any concerns.

Group Rates

We encourage firms to take advantage of our group discount through room block purchases. Contact us to find out more.

Registration fees for Into the Woods are not refundable, however, they are transferable. If you decide not to attend after registering, you may find another attendee to take your place.
